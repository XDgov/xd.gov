How to Apply

To apply for this position, please send your resume and cover letter to HRD.Schedule.A.Expert.Intern.Fellows.Hiring@census.gov.

Resume Format

We recommend formatting your resume in a way that highlights any and all experience you have in data science, IT, software development, and the management of technology projects. We also recommend following the guidance published by both USAJobs and the Technology Transformation Service (TTS) to ensure your application recieves the fullest consideration.

For more information about the Emerging Technology Fellowship, please visit the official Census webpage.

If you have additional questions, please reach out to inquiries@xd.gov
